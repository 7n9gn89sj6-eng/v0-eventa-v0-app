## How to Use Eventa

### 1. **Search for Events** (No Sign-In Required)

Just type or say what you're looking for in plain language:

**Examples:**
- "jazz concerts in Paris this weekend"
- "food markets in Rome in April"
- "I'm going to Berlin for a week and like music and food"
- "things happening near me tonight"
- "community events in Athens"

**What Eventa does:**
- Understands your question in natural language
- Extracts location, date, and interests automatically
- Shows you relevant events, with community events first

**Try it:**
- Search in your preferred language
- Use trip-style queries (e.g., "I'm going to...")
- Test location detection (click the map pin icon)
- Mix and match: location + category + date

---

### 2. **Browse Events**

- Click "Browse" in the header to see all events
- Use filters (date, category, city) to narrow results
- Click any event to see full details
- Export events to your calendar (.ics file)

---

### 3. **Create an Event** (Sign-In Required)

If you'd like to post an event:

1. Click "Post Event" on the homepage
2. Sign in with your email (magic link, no password needed)
3. Fill out the event form:
   - Title and description
   - Date and time
   - Location (address or venue name)
   - Category
   - Price (free or paid)
4. Submit your event
5. Check your email for an edit link (you can edit without logging in again!)

**Note**: Events go through AI moderation for safety. Most are approved automatically, but some may need human review.
