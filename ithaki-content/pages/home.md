# Home

This site brings together curated journeys rooted in Hellas (Greece), along with writing and conversations shaped by them.

The work here is grounded in slow travel — by walking, listening, and paying attention to place, history, and the living connection between past and present.

While Hellas remains the centre of gravity, the journeys and reflections gathered here extend across the wider southern European world shaped by Hellenic movement and exchange — from the western Mediterranean to the Black Sea. What matters is not borders, but continuity: how ideas, routes, and ways of living have travelled, settled, and endured.
